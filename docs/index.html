Interactive guide for determining when to use AI in software development

New Development Task

Let's determine the best approach for your development task.

Is this a well-defined task with existing patterns?
Is it novel/complex requiring new architectural decisions?
Does it involve sensitive data or security-critical functions?
Choose your security approach:
Task Complexity Assessment:
AI-FIRST Approach Recommended

High suitability for AI generation

This task is well-suited for AI to handle the initial implementation. AI can effectively generate boilerplate code, CRUD operations, tests, and other repetitive patterns.

AI-ASSISTED Approach Recommended

Collaborative approach works best

Use AI to accelerate development while maintaining human oversight for business logic, integrations, and architectural decisions.

HUMAN-FIRST Approach Recommended

Human expertise should lead

This task requires human expertise for novel algorithms, complex architecture, or security-critical functionality. Use AI for research assistance.

MIXED Approach Recommended

Balanced exploration and structure

Use AI for exploration and research, but rely on human expertise for structural and architectural decisions.

LOCAL AI Recommended

Security-conscious AI usage

Use local AI models to maintain data privacy while still benefiting from AI assistance in development.

ENHANCED REVIEW Process

Extra security validation required

Standard AI assistance with mandatory security team review and enhanced testing protocols.
